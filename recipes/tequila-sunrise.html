Tequila Sunrise

The tequila sunrise is a cocktail made of tequila, orange juice, and grenadine syrup.

The drink is served unmixed in a tall glass. And it looks awesome.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. tequila
  • 38 oz. sunrise
  • 2 oz. orange juice (fresh, please)
  • 1/2 oz. grenadine
  • Garnish: Fruits!

Steps

  1. Pour the tequila in a long glass full of ice
  2. Pour the orange juice like you don't care
  3. This is the tricky part, slowly but firmly pour the grenadine against one of the edges of the glass and watch it crawl down the bottom
  4. Behold the sunrise, you're a bartender now

Warning: May not taste like it, but this is an alcoholic beverage.
